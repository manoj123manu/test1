acquisition & retention emails still works as the best platform.
A well structured email or SMS campaign has a very high success rate. The receiver is likely to forward to others if it
creates the right interest. It has the advantage of going into the personal box.

What do we do to achieve your goals?

  • The database is chosen after high scrutiny.
  • The campaigns are monitored according to the Opening rates, click through rates & visits to the promo landing page or leads in the case of emails.
  • We ensure the database is used prudently to avoid spam.

In many cases, emails or SMS works instantly especially during festive seasons.
